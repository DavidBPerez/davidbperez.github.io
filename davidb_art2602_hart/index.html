<Caption>Codebased Drawing ~David B.

Hi I'm David! This is my HTML coded artwork I did for this project! The inspiration behind this HTML painting is Wassily Kandinsky's "Several Circles", 1962 [ref. via text link] which is comprised of 'several circles' as seen here. Since the piece was a geometrical abstract it was easily do-able in html, but sadly couldn't recapture the texture or detail of the piece due to certain limitations. Overall though all-in-all this recreation in uses several layered 'div' objects to recapture the essential essence of the piece spanning to atleast 1402 lines of HTML code! :D
